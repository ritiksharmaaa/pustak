# Remarkable Inventions

The history of humankind is shaped by a few remarkable inventions.

- The wheel changed transportation and made trade easier.
- The printing press spread knowledge and transformed education.
- Electricity powered modern life and unlocked new technologies.
- The internet connected the world and changed how we live, learn, and work.
